SpoonRocket Data Analysis

I assist SpoonRocket with data analysis as a finance intern. Utilizing primarily PostgreSQL and Excel, I influenced decisions concerning order forecasting, driver scheduling, and customer acquisition. I also created models to track and analyze driver sessions and sold out times of items. I am currently analyzing user preferences and trends for more accurate forecasting metrics.
